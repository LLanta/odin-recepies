Lasagna

Description

Simple lasagne recipe. Delisious dish waiting for you to make !

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheet
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Oil

  2. Oil the baking sheet
  3. Place lasagna sheets on pan
  4. Add sauce
  5. Place another row od lasagna sheets
  6. Put dish in the oven
